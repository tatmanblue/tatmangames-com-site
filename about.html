Founded in 2018 to build exciting and dynamic desktop games, Tatman Games has been quietly working under the radar.

For the first few years the focus has been building engines that simulate life-like economic behavior while hosted in a closed systems.

On Oct 23, 2023 Tatman Games released the first of those games, Max Capacity.

Tatman Games is founded by Matt Raffel (), a 30 yr software engineering veteran, with most of his experience in delivering commercial software products in FinTech.
